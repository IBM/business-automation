## What is IBM Business Automation?

In a world driven by processes, chaos is the silent killer of efficiency…

**Business Automation** technology is designed to tackle this chaos head on.

With technology applied in both front and back offices to: <br>
- **Perform repetitive tasks** <br>
- **Make adaptive decisions** <br>
- **Manage enterprise data** <br>
- **Optimize processes** <br>
- With **minimal human intervention** <br>
